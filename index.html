ABOUT US


CocoLanguage is a language learning website consisting of tutorial quizzes to help the users be familiarized with some of the basic words of the Japanese Language.The best way to learn a language is from repetitive listening and practicing. With this principle in mind, the creators of this website hope that itâ€™s users can learn some of the important words of the Japanese Language which you can use during conversations. The principal intent of this website is to provide a simple website to be accessed by someone who is travelling and wishes to know some of the basic words in order to communicate with the natives of the country, Japan. Furthermore, this website is also for those who wanted to exercise their brain or for those who just wanted to learn something new.CocoLanguage will not support a wide range of words. It will be limited to the basics for now and will probably be developed further in the future. Quizzes will be provided to help the users learn words of different categories such as greetings, person, town and basics.
